My Objective

My service learning goal for Church and Global Cultures is to critically reflect on disability culture, church engagement with it and the values of encounter and transformation

My Goals

Along with my service learning goal I am also striving to achieve these two personal goals.

  • Goal 1 To strengthen my understanding how the Roman Catholic Church engages with the rest of the world on an economic level.
  • Goal 2 To raise my awareness on how the Roman Catholic Church affects other cultures around the world.
